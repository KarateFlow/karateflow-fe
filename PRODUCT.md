# Product

## Register

product

## Platform

web

## Users
Karate instructors and coaches evaluating athletes during training or grading.

## Product Purpose
To standardize and track karate tests/grading, making evaluations objective and easy to report. Success looks like coaches being able to quickly and accurately grade athletes and track their progress over time.

## Positioning
It provides a specialized, digital testing and evaluation framework specifically for karate athletes.

## Brand Personality
Professional, clean, and disciplined. Focuses on clarity and speed, drawing inspiration from minimalist, workflow-focused tools like Linear or Notion.

## Anti-references
No overly aggressive "gym bro" designs (neon colors, extreme bold fonts) and no childish gamification or badges.

## Design Principles
- Practice what you preach: The interface should feel as disciplined and structured as the martial art it evaluates.
- Clarity over decoration: Ensure data and evaluations are easy to read and enter quickly.
- Focus on the athlete: The interface should stay out of the way, allowing the coach to focus on the athlete's performance without distraction.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Standard web accessibility, ensuring clear contrast and full keyboard navigation.
